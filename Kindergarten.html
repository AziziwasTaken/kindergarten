About the Brainiacs

Brainiac Kids Preparatory Academy is a Junior and senior preschool program for children age 2-5. We attempt to provide your child with the knowledge they will need to prosper when entering secondary school.

Brainiac Kids was found by Chelsea Mitchell a well qualified woman with experience with children. This expereience comes from being previously employed as a teacher at another kindergarten. Ms Mitchell left her previous position becasue she saw that in her vision a greater kindergarten experience could be provided.
